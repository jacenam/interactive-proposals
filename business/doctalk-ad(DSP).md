## [Business Strategy] Doctalk Ads: The Only Data-Driven DSP & DMP Dominating the $8B Healthcare Ad Market

### 1. Overview

The South Korean healthcare advertising market is valued at approximately **$8 billion (USD)**, with the online segment accounting for **$5 billion**. Leading digital platforms — such as Naver, Kakao, Daangn, and Gangnam Unni — serve as the primary channels for patient acquisition.

While medical practitioners are experts in their clinical fields, they often find the complexities of digital advertising overwhelming. Consequently, most medical institutions outsource their ad strategy, creative production, and execution to agencies.

### 2. The Problem: "The Data Blind Spot"

Traditional advertising agencies face a fundamental limitation in the healthcare sector: **Data Disconnection**.

- **Fragmented Metrics:** Agencies can track "top-of-funnel" data like impressions and clicks provided by platforms.
- **Invisible Conversion:** They cannot verify whether a click actually resulted in a physical hospital visit. This lack of "bottom-of-funnel" data makes it impossible to calculate true ROI or optimize for actual patient conversion.

### 3. The Solution: Full-Cycle Performance Tracking

Doctalk Ads bridges this gap by leveraging the **Doctalk Medical Appointment** infrastructure to provide **Full-Cycle Tracking** from initial ad exposure to the final clinical consultation.

- **Integrated Booking Ecosystem:** Doctalk is already the official booking intermediary for Korea's largest platforms (Naver, Kakao, etc.), allowing us to identify the exact entry point of every patient.
- **EMR (Electronic Medical Record) Sync:** Our deep integration with hospital EMR systems allows us to track real-time outcomes. We know if a patient actually arrived, cancelled, or was a "no-show."
- **Outcome-Driven ROAS:** By consolidating visit data with ad spend, we provide medical advertisers with a verified **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**, enabling them to optimize budgets based on actual revenue rather than mere clicks.

### 4. How It Works: Specialized Healthcare DSP & DMP

Docfriends is evolving beyond a service provider into a dominant **Healthcare-Specific DSP (Demand Side Platform)** and **DMP (Data Management Platform)**.

#### A. The Specialized DSP Model

Major tech platforms operate across diverse categories (dining, beauty, retail) and lack the specialized sales force to manage tens of thousands of individual medical practitioners. Doctalk fills this gap by:

- Aggregating medical ad inventory in **bulk** and supplying it to major platforms.
- Acting as the primary healthcare ad specialist, securing a sustainable **Revenue Share (RS) of 15% to 30%**.

#### B. The Unique DMP Advantage

Through Doctalk Medical Appointment, we possess exclusive data on **who** (age, gender), **where** (region), and **what** (medical specialty) patients are searching for and visiting.

- By mining and refining this "High-Intent" data, we function as a **DMP** that offers unprecedented targeting precision, making us the only player capable of delivering data-verified healthcare performance marketing.

### 5. The Growth Flywheel

Our business model is built on a self-sustaining **Flywheel Effect** designed for exponential growth:

1. **Smart Ads:** Data-driven targeting increases booking rates.
2. **Provider Retention:** High conversion leads to more hospitals joining the Doctalk ecosystem.
3. **Data Accumulation:** Increased participation generates more granular booking and EMR data.
4. **System Optimization:** More data refines our AI algorithms, further lowering acquisition costs and increasing ROAS.

> **Conclusion:** Doctalk Ads is uniquely positioned to dominate the **$8 billion healthcare market** by transforming "clicks" into "confirmed patients." We are not just an ad platform; we are the data backbone of the modern healthcare economy.

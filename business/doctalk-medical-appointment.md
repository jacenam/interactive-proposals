## [Service Overview] Doctalk Appointment

Docfriends is revolutionizing the healthcare advertising and reservation market by integrating fragmented Electronic Medical Record (EMR) systems with major consumer platforms. We bridge the gap between digital discovery and physical clinical visits through data-driven solutions.

### 1. Overview

Traditionally, hospital reservation systems in Korea rely on diverse, non-compatible EMRs, creating fragmented data silos across the medical industry. Doctalk was built to solve this by seamlessly connecting major consumer platforms directly to hospital EMRs.

### 2. The Problem: Fragmentation and Inefficiency

- **System Fragmentation:** Existing hospital reservation systems are hindered by diverse, non-compatible EMRs, leading to manual data entry and "data silos."
- **Operational Bottleneck:** Staff must manually manage appointments from multiple channels, resulting in high administrative costs and frequent human errors.

### 3. The Solution: Seamless Integration

- **Unified Interface:** Doctalk connects major platforms — **Naver, Daangn, Kakao, and Gangnam Unni** — directly to the hospital's EMR.
- **Resource Optimization:** By managing all external reservations through a single EMR dashboard, clinics significantly reduce operational overhead and eliminate redundant tasks.

### 4. How It Works: Product Core Features

#### Real-time Slot Synchronization (The "Heart" of Doctalk)

Managing online and offline patients simultaneously is a major pain point. Doctalk solves this by:

- **Live Sync:** When a patient is registered via phone or walk-in in the EMR, the available "slots" on Naver, Kakao, and Daangn are updated instantly.
- **Example:** If a clinic sets a limit of 3 patients per 30 minutes and 2 walk-ins are recorded, the online platforms will automatically show "1 slot remaining" or "Closed" once the limit is reached.

#### One-Stop Product & Schedule Management

- **Universal Update:** Create a service item (e.g., "Lifting Treatment") once in Doctalk, and it reflects across all linked platforms simultaneously.
- **Granular Control:** Easily configure doctor-specific off-days, lunch hours, and "Instant Confirmation" settings without accessing multiple portals.

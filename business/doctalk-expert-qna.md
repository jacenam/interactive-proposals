A systematic review process is conducted to ensure legally compliant and high-quality content publication, in accordance with medical advertising law.

1. **Keyword Strategy Collection:** Select optimal keywords based on target patient region, age, illness, and medical specialty
2. **Content Composition:** Experts compose questions and answers based on professional medical knowledge (including medical information and content with editorial guidelines)

4. **Auto-publish & Management:** After auto-uploading to Naver Knowledge iN, Doctalk AI systems provide ongoing quality management and responsive corrections

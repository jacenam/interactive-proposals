## Doctalk AI Medical Advertising Auto-Review

Doctalk AI Medical Advertising Auto-Review is a core middleware service designed to bridge the gap between **Doctalk Reservation** and **Doctalk Advertising**. By resolving the chronic bottlenecks in the traditional South Korean medical advertising review process, this service accelerates the supply of medical advertisements to online platforms and drives the growth of the Doctalk advertising ecosystem.

### 1. Overview

Traditionally, the medical advertising review process in Korea was plagued by labor shortages, leading to severe delays.

### 2. The Problem

Review wait times typically lasted **2 to 4 months**, causing significant delays in promoting new medical procedures and processing financial settlements.

### 3. The Solution

Developed by **Docfriends** in official partnership with the **Korean Medical Association (KMA)**, this AI system allows medical institutions to request reviews instantly. It enables the KMA to process high volumes of advertising without exhausting human resources.

The AI model is built upon a vast, exhaustive dataset to ensure legal compliance and professional accuracy:

- **Official Guidelines:** Comprehensive guidelines from the Korean Medical Association (KMA) and the Ministry of Health and Welfare (MOHW).
- **Legal Frameworks:** Medical Law (Enforcement Decree), Intellectual Property Rights, and Personal Information Protection Act guidelines.
- **Legal Precedents:** 5 major Supreme Court rulings and administrative interpretations from regional public health centers.
- **Proprietary Expertise:** Analysis data from over 40 medical law conferences hosted by Docfriends over the past 7 years.

### 4. Operational Workflow (Human-in-the-Loop)

The system employs an intuitive and legally sound "AI-Assisted" flow. Rather than making a final, autonomous legal judgment, the AI acts as a sophisticated decision-support tool for human reviewers.

#### The Review Flow:

1. **Submission & Instant Analysis:** When a clinic submits an ad creative (text, image, or video), the AI instantly scans for potential violations.
2. **Detailed AI Feedback:** The system provides an "Approval" or "Rejection" opinion, accompanied by **specific, detailed reasons** and relevant legal clauses.
3. **Human Decision-Making:** These AI-generated insights are forwarded to the KMA medical advertising review officer. The **final decision** is made by the human officer, ensuring professional accountability.
4. **Virtuous Data Cycle:** As more reviews are processed, the AI learns from the human officer's final decisions, leading to a "Flywheel Effect" where the accuracy and speed of the AI improve continuously.

This service represents the first domestic case of total automation in the medical advertising review pipeline, significantly reducing administrative burdens.

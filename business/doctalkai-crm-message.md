## [Service Overview] Doctalk AI CRM Message

### 1. Overview

Most clinics use basic EMR (Electronic Medical Record) features for patient messaging, but behind the scenes, staff face an enormous amount of hidden labor and fatigue in the process.

### 2. The Problem: "Labor-Intensive Spam"

- **Operational Overload:** Every afternoon, all staff gather to discuss "who to send messages to, what content to include," then manually compose and send — severely hindering operational efficiency. (Before clocking out, spending over 1.5 hours on messaging alone is truly exhausting!)
- **Low Delivery Impact:** From the patient's perspective, receiving a generic template message that doesn't match their situation feels less like "someone caring for me" and more like **"advertising spam."**
- **High Entry Barriers:** Existing CRM services (Revi, Afterdoc, etc.) exist, but staff end up manually creating templates and configuring scenarios — stuck in **"passive automation."**

### 3. The Solution: "Data-Driven Personalization"

Doctalk AI fundamentally solves this problem through its partnership with **Bit Computer**, Korea's leading EMR company, and other major EMR providers.

#### A. Doctor-Specific RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) Model

- **Protecting Provider Identity:** Doctors are uncomfortable when their clinical style is learned by a third party or when advice doesn't reflect their approach. Doctalk AI builds a **doctor-specific individual RAG** to generate messages that carry each provider's unique clinical philosophy and prescription style.
- **De-identified Data Utilization:** While protecting personal information, the system analyzes actual clinical data — treatments, prescriptions, examinations, memos — to provide the most accurate guide.

#### B. Optimal Targeting and Timing Recommendations

- **Who & When:** AI determines "Who is the patient that should receive a message right now, at this moment?"
- **What:** AI considers "Given this patient's examination results and past medical history, what advice is most needed?" and recommends the message accordingly.
- **Outcome:** For medical staff, the workflow is simplified to just pressing the "Confirm" button. For patients, they experience **"a personal doctor who truly cares about me."**

### 4. How It Works: Doctalk AI vs Traditional CRM

| Category | Traditional CRM (Revi, Afterdoc, etc.) | Doctalk AI Patient Message |
|---|---|---|
| Data Utilization | User-configured scenario-based | Based on actual treatment/prescription/examination data |
| Message Generation | Pre-defined template selection | Doctor-specific RAG-based personalized generation |
| Operational Effort | Initial setup and template management required | AI auto-recommendation (minimal confirmation) |
| Patient Experience | Generic hospital notification text | Personalized care message from "my doctor who remembers me" |

### 5. Future Scalability & Vision: "Completing the Medical My Data"

Doctalk AI does not stop at simply sending text messages.

1. **Multi-channel Expansion:** Expand beyond Alim Talk and Naver Talk Talk to Daangn, Blind, KidsNote, and other platforms where patients primarily engage, maximizing re-engagement through **AD Notification** formats.
2. **EMR Partnership Expansion:** Starting with Bit Computer, build a data ecosystem through alliances with major EMR companies.
3. **Solving the Government's Unfinished Challenge:** While the government-led "Medical My Data" initiative struggled due to lack of EMR company participation, Docfriends takes a **field-driven business model** approach — combining actual doctor-patient data to become the first to practically deliver on what that initiative could not.

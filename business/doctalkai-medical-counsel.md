## [Business Strategy Report] EMR-Based AI Medical Navigation & Platform Expansion Model

### 1. Overview

The majority of patients experience severe information gaps and inefficiencies when symptoms arise or when post-treatment management is needed.

### 2. The Problem: "The Medical Maze"

- **Lack of Personal Network:** Except for the few who have a doctor acquaintance, patients have no way to judge the severity of their symptoms — whether it's serious or mild, or which specialist to see.
- **Confusion in Choosing a Provider:** Even when a specific symptom requires specialized diagnostic equipment or a particular **subspecialist**, patients have no way to know, wasting time choosing between clinics (1st, 2nd, 3rd choice) randomly.
- **Fragmented Information & Post-care Gaps:** Post-treatment concerns (prescription side effects, test result interpretation, etc.) are burdensome to resolve by revisiting the hospital. Internet searches have low reliability, creating a constant state of anxiety.

### 3. The Solution: Organic Combination of [Doctalk AI CRM Message] and [AI Medical Consultation]

Doctalk AI connects every moment of the patient's journey — from leaving the hospital to returning again — through data, using **"message"** as the trigger.

#### Phase 1. Trigger: Doctalk AI CRM Message (CRM's Perspective)

- **EMR-based Auto-delivery:** Linked to EMR data such as completed treatments, test results received, and appointment reminders, Alim Talk/text messages are automatically sent to patients.
- **Personalized Consultation Link:** At the bottom of each message, a **1:1 AI consultation link** connected to the patient's treatment history is attached. This goes beyond simple guidance — it instantly resolves patient concerns, extending the conversation to a **"dedicated nurse"** or **"doctor acquaintance."**

#### Phase 2. Consultation: RAG-Based AI Medical Agent (The Role of a Doctor Acquaintance)

- **Data-driven Consultation:** When the patient clicks the link, the AI agent responds using the patient's EMR records (treatments, prescriptions, test results, etc.) via RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology.
- **High-reliability Information:** Within the scope of medical law, the agent provides kind and accurate explanations of symptom causes, test result meanings, and follow-up precautions — delivering the experience of asking a trusted **"doctor acquaintance."**

#### Phase 3. Conversion: Smart Visit Guide & Real-time Appointment

- **Triage & Matching:** When consultation results indicate that an in-person examination or further testing is needed, AI recommends the optimal **specialist and facility equipped with the necessary diagnostic equipment**.
- **Real-time Slot Booking:** Simultaneously links to the recommended hospital's real-time appointment system (available slots), allowing patients to schedule a visit at their desired time immediately.

#### Phase 4. Feedback: Maximizing Treatment Efficiency (Pre-Examination Summary)

- **Assetizing Consultation Content:** AI-to-patient consultation content is summarized and recorded in the provider's EMR as a pre-examination document. Doctors fully understand the patient's condition through the pre-consultation AI summary, elevating the quality of care.

### 4. How It Works: API-Based Platform Business

Doctalk AI's value extends beyond individual hospital services — it scales strategically through API integration with major platforms like Naver, Kakao, Daangn, and Gangnam Unni.

- **"Consultation Matching" from Search:** When users describe symptoms on partner platforms, the Doctalk AI engine on the back-end analyzes EMR partner data to match the optimal medical provider.
- **Platform Win-Win Structure:** Partner platforms provide users with a highly refined consultation-to-appointment UX, while Docfriends supplies the available slot data and specialist matching logic via API.
- **New Advertising Model:** In compliance with medical advertising law, Docfriends develops a **"consultation-linked targeted ad"** layer to maximize conversion, sharing the resulting revenue with platform partners.

### 5. Expected Impact & Conclusion

- **Patient Side:** Receive accurate guidance based on medical records without needing a "doctor acquaintance," ensuring **healthcare sovereignty** — visiting the right hospital without wandering.
- **Hospital Side:** Complete a virtuous cycle of patient Retention (via Doctalk AI CRM Message) and new patient Acquisition (via platform API).
- **Industry Side:** Integrate fragmented medical data through AI, building a **data-centric medical ecosystem** where patients, hospitals, and platforms all thrive.
